Overview of Requests


Approved Pending Off Holiday Rework    
Enter the required data for filing a leave and click on 'Submit' button.
Type:
1. Filing of vacation leave should be done at least one (1) day in advance before the actual date of leave.
2. Filing of VL should only be halfday or whole day only. Please visit FAQs' tab to view the halfday schedule. It can be 4 hours, 8 hours, 4.8 hours or 9.6 hours.
3. Enter date as mm/dd/yyyy (e.g. 03/02/2010 for March 3, 2010).
4. You can input either time or duration (actual leave). You can type/enter time format in military format (e.g. 0800 to 1830). This will automatically convert to the set system format (e.g. 4:00 AM to 6:30 PM). Duration refers to the actual leave hours; enter either your equivalent halfday or whole day leave.
5. “Notes" is required.
